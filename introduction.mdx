## What is AI Studio?

AI Studio is an AI-powered real estate photo editing and video creation platform designed for real estate professionals. Upload property photos, apply professional style templates, and generate marketing-ready visuals in seconds.

The platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with an intuitive workflow, allowing you to:

- **Transform photos** with AI-powered style templates
- **Create property videos** with automated transitions and music
- **Collaborate with teams** in shared workspaces
- **Manage projects** from dashboard to delivery

## Core features

### AI photo enhancement

Upload property photos and apply professionally curated style templates. The platform uses Fal.ai's image generation models to transform your images while preserving architectural elements like windows, walls, and doors.

**Available style categories:**
- **Staging** - Modern, Scandinavian, and minimalist interior designs
- **Lighting** - Enhanced natural and ambient lighting (coming soon)
- **Exterior** - Curb appeal and landscape improvements (coming soon)
- **Atmosphere** - Seasonal and mood-based transformations (coming soon)

### Video project creation

Create property tour videos by combining your enhanced images into 5-second clips with smooth transitions:

- **Aspect ratios**: 16:9 (landscape), 9:16 (portrait), or 1:1 (square)
- **Music tracks**: Pre-curated royalty-free background music
- **AI motion**: Kling AI generates dynamic camera movements
- **Transitions**: Seamless blends or clean cuts between clips

### Team workspaces

Organize your work in collaborative workspaces:

- **Role-based access**: Owner, admin, and member permissions
- **Shared projects**: All team members access the same projects
- **Team invitations**: Invite members via email
- **White-label branding**: Custom logo and colors for agencies

### Project management

Track all your photo and video projects in one dashboard:

- **Grid and table views** for easy navigation
- **Status tracking**: pending, processing, completed, or failed
- **Before/after comparisons** for image projects
- **Batch operations** across multiple images

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up and onboard">
    Create your account, set up your workspace, and complete the onboarding form with your organization details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Click "New Project" in the dashboard, upload your property photos, and select a style template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI processing">
    The platform sends your images to Fal.ai for AI enhancement. Processing typically takes 30-60 seconds per image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and download">
    View your enhanced images in the project detail page. Download individual images or the entire project as a ZIP file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  AI Studio preserves architectural elements during photo enhancement. Windows, walls, doors, and room layouts remain exactly as shown in your original images.
</Note>

## Tech stack

AI Studio is built with modern web technologies:

- **Next.js 16** - React framework with App Router and Server Actions
- **PostgreSQL + Drizzle ORM** - Type-safe database operations
- **Fal.ai** - AI-powered image generation and editing
- **Kling AI** - Video clip generation with motion
- **Trigger.dev** - Background job processing
- **Better Auth** - Authentication with OAuth providers
- **Supabase** - File storage and database hosting
- **Tailwind CSS v4** - Utility-first styling
